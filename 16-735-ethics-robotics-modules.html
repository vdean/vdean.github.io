Ethics and Robotics Module Projects

The course culminated in ethics module design projects in collaboration with instructors of 11 other CMU computer sicence and robotics courses. Pairs of students created ethics intervention modules tailored to each course and instructor. We look forward to these modules being implemented in their courses in future semesters!
